BMW has a rich history in motorsports, and over the years, they’ve produced some iconic race cars. Here’s a list of 10 of the best and most influential BMW race cars, spanning various categories and eras:

1. BMW 328 Mille Miglia (1936)

Category: Sports Car Highlights: The BMW 328 was a revolutionary car for its time, with lightweight construction and advanced aerodynamics. It won the Mille Miglia in 1940, securing BMW's reputation in motorsport.

2. BMW 3.0 CSL (1972)

Category: Touring Car / Endurance Highlights: Known as the "Batmobile" for its distinctive aerodynamic wing and aggressive design, the 3.0 CSL was one of BMW’s most successful touring cars, with multiple wins in the European Touring Car Championship and the 24 Hours of Spa.

3.BMW M1 (1978)

Category: Supercar / Group 4 Racing Highlights: The BMW M1 was a collaboration with Lamborghini and is BMW's first supercar. It also competed in the IMSA GTX series and Group 4 racing, with its mid-engine layout and iconic styling.

4. BMW V12 LMR (1999)

Category: Prototype / Endurance Highlights: This car was designed to race in the 24 Hours of Le Mans and won the prestigious event in 1999. The V12 LMR had a V12 engine that produced 580 hp and showcased BMW’s advanced engineering in endurance racing.

5. BMW E30 M3 (1986)

Category: Touring Car Highlights: The E30 M3 is an icon in motorsport and was incredibly successful in Group A racing, winning multiple championships, including the World Touring Car Championship and European Touring Car Championship.

6. BMW Z4 GT3 (2010)

Category: GT Racing Highlights: The BMW Z4 GT3 became one of the most dominant cars in GT racing, achieving wins in the 24 Hours of Nurburgring and various other endurance series. It’s known for its balance, performance, and reliability.

7. BMW M6 GT3 (2016)

Category: GT Racing Highlights: Based on the BMW M6, this car competed in endurance racing, including the Blancpain GT Series. It featured a 4.4-liter V8 and had a successful career with numerous podium finishes.

8. BMW 2002 Turbo (1973)

Category: Touring / Rally Car Highlights: The BMW 2002 Turbo was a pioneer in the use of turbocharging in production cars and also had a successful career in rallying and touring car racing. Its nimble handling and turbocharged engine made it a legend.

9. BMW M4 DTM (2014)

Category: DTM (Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters) Highlights: The BMW M4 DTM was a purpose-built race car for the DTM series and has been a dominant force in the competition. It won multiple races and helped BMW secure the 2014 DTM manufacturer’s championship.

10. BMW 320i (E21) (1977)

Category: Touring Car Highlights: The BMW 320i was the car that laid the foundation for BMW's success in touring car racing. It won the European Touring Car Championship in 1983 and helped establish the BMW brand in motorsports.
