1. BMW E46 M3 (2000–2006)

Why it's great: Legendary performance, precision handling, and timeless design.
Engine: 3.2L inline-6 (S54)
Transmission: Manual or SMG
Still loved by enthusiasts for its raw driving feel.

2. BMW F80 M3 / F82 M4 (2014–2019)

Why it’s great: Powerful turbocharged engine, advanced tech, daily usability.
Engine: 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 (S55)
Performance: Up to 444 hp (Competition Package)

3. BMW E39 5 Series (1995–2004) – Especially the M5

Why it’s great: Considered one of the best-built BMWs ever.
M5 Version: 4.9L V8, 400 hp
Known for: Perfect balance of luxury and sport

4. BMW X5 (G05, 2019–present)

Why it’s great: Luxury SUV with sports car dynamics
Tech-loaded, spacious, and fast (especially the M50i or X5 M versions)

5. BMW i4 M50 (2021–present)

Why it’s great: Best-driving electric BMW yet
Electric power + M performance
Range: Up to 270 miles
0–60 mph: Around 3.7 seconds

6. BMW E30 M3 (1986–1992)

Why it’s great: The original M3 that started it all
Engine: 2.3L inline-4 (S14)
Motorsport heritage: Built for homologation in touring car racing
Legacy: Light, analog, and razor-sharp—now a collector’s gem

7. BMW G80 M3 / G82 M4 (2021–present)

Why it’s great: Modern M power with AWD option and tech-loaded cabin
Engine: 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 (S58)
Power: Up to 503 hp (Competition xDrive)
Note: Controversial grille, but performance is undeniably elite

8. BMW E38 7 Series (1994–2001)

Why it’s great: Quintessential luxury sedan of the '90s
Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, and V12
Known for: Understated elegance, rock-solid engineering, and James Bond approval (used in Tomorrow Never Dies)

9. BMW Z3 M Coupe (1998–2002)

Why it’s great: Cult classic with unique shooting-brake design
Engine: 3.2L inline-6 (S52 or S54 in later models)
Nickname: "Clown Shoe" – quirky looks but exceptional driving experience

10. BMW i8 (2014–2020)

Why it’s great: A futuristic plug-in hybrid supercar
Powertrain: 1.5L turbo 3-cylinder + electric motor
0–60 mph: ~4.2 seconds
Unique for: Combining exotic design with sustainability focus
